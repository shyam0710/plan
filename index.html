TradeLyser Digital Marketing Plan

TradeLyser is not a “nice to have” product. It is a behavior change platform. We are not selling software. We are selling control, clarity, and consistency.

Discipline is the edge
Your data is the truth
Feels like a coach

Big Picture Strategy

This is a behavior change product. The plan shifts trader mindset, proves value through data, and positions TradeLyser as a coach that builds discipline.

Education first
Trust-led
Activation later

Positioning

  • Not software. Control, clarity, and consistency.
  • Discipline matters more than indicators.
  • TradeLyser feels like a coach, not a tool.
Core message: Discipline is the edge. Your data tells the truth.

6-Month Path

  • Month 1: Foundation and authority
  • Month 2: Education + product awareness
  • Month 3: Activation + trials
  • Month 4: Community + retention
  • Month 5: Scale via partnerships
  • Month 6: Brand authority + PR
1

Month 1: Foundation + Positioning (Launch Month)

Establish brand, authority and clarity. No aggressive selling. Only education and trust.

SEO setup
Authority content
No paid ads

SEO

  • Title/meta + H1/H2 structure for core pages
  • Sitemap.xml + robots.txt
  • Schema: SaaS product + FAQs
  • Page speed + internal linking

Keyword buckets: trading journal, trading journal india, options trading journal, zerodha trade tracking, trading analytics, trading discipline, trading journal excel

SEO Content

  • What is a trading journal and why most traders fail without one
  • Why discipline matters more than indicators
  • Best trading journal for Indian traders
  • How to review your trades like a professional
Goal is indexing and relevance, not traffic.

Social Media

  • Platforms: Instagram, YouTube Shorts, X, Facebook
  • Instagram: 4 reels/week
  • YouTube Shorts: 4/week
  • X: 1 post/day
  • Facebook: 3/week

Founder + Influencers

  • Founder Voice Series: 2 videos/week, 3 to 5 minutes
  • Topics: journey, why TradeLyser, psychology, discipline frameworks
  • Influencers: identify 30 creators and engage (no paid deals yet)
Paid ads: none. Do not spend before message-market fit.
2

Month 2: Education + Product Awareness

Make traders aware that a better way exists. Education stays first, product becomes visible.

Blogs
Comparisons
Retargeting

SEO

  • Publish 6 blog posts
  • 2 comparison pages: TradeLyser vs Excel, TradeLyser vs TraderSync
  • Start internal linking

Social Media

  • Instagram + YouTube: 5 reels/week
  • X: 2 posts/day + 1 thread/week
  • Add screen recordings: board, tagging, AI insights
Education first, product second.

Founder Content

  • Weekly Trading Talk: 10 to 15 mins
  • Cut into 5 to 7 shorts
  • Topics: overtrading, emotions, pro reviews, what data reveals

Soft CTA: “If you are serious about improving, tools like TradeLyser exist for this exact reason.”

Influencers + Ads

  • Outreach 10 micro influencers (10k to 50k)
  • Offer free pro access, early features, revenue share
  • Paid ads: retargeting only, small test budget
3

Month 3: Activation + Trial Push

Drive trials and broker connections. Stronger product focus with proof-driven stories.

Broker pages
Cold traffic

SEO

  • Publish 8 blogs
  • Broker pages: Zerodha, Groww, Angel One
Broker pages convert well because intent is specific.

Social Mix

  • 40% education
  • 40% product use cases
  • 20% testimonials and stories

Influencer Demo Campaign

  • 1 full demo video
  • 2 short clips + 1 story set
  • Message: “This is how I review my trades now.”

Paid Ads

  • Start cold traffic: Google Search + Meta Reels
  • Keywords: trading journal, options trading journal, trading analytics, zerodha journal
  • Creatives: problem-based, not feature-based
4

Month 4: Community + Retention

Turn users into loyal users by building discipline habits and visible progress.

Challenge
Habit loop

Discipline Challenge

  • 4-week challenge: journal daily, tag trades, max loss rule
  • Weekly reviews
  • Rewards: free month, feature access, public recognition
Builds habit and identity, drives retention.

Social Content

  • Challenge updates
  • User progress stories
  • Behind-the-scenes

Podcast

  • User journey reviews
  • Mistakes fixed using discipline

SEO + Influencers

  • Publish case studies and user stories
  • Get influencers to join the challenge publicly
5

Month 5: Scale + Partnerships

Lower CAC through partnerships and mentor distribution. Scale only what already works.

Partners
Mentors

Broker Partnerships

  • Co-webinars
  • Co-email campaigns
  • In-app placements
Message: “Trade better. Trade disciplined.”

Mentor Program

  • Target 20 mentors
  • Provide mentor dashboard, codes, marketing kit
  • Mentors bring mentees

Influencers

  • Sign 3 larger creators
  • Long-term contracts

Paid Ads

  • Increase budget only on winning creatives
  • Kill everything else
6

Month 6: Brand Authority + PR

Make TradeLyser a category name. Own trading discipline as a conversation in India.

PR
Podcast

PR + Media

  • Founder interviews
  • Articles: discipline, AI in trading, Indian trader psychology
Objective: category association with discipline + performance analytics.

TradeLyser Podcast

  • Weekly episodes
  • Guests: traders, mentors, psychologists, market experts
  • Repurpose into shorts

Authority Outcomes

  • More branded searches
  • Higher trial conversion due to trust
  • Mentor and creator ecosystem becomes a growth engine

Category Goal

TradeLyser becomes the default name in the “trading discipline” conversation, driven by proof, community, and repeatable distribution.
