Big Picture Strategy

TradeLyser changes behavior. The plan is built to shift mindset, prove value through data, and position TradeLyser as a coach that builds discipline.

Educate
Build trust
Activate trials

Positioning

  • Not software. Control, clarity, and consistency.
  • Discipline matters more than indicators.
  • TradeLyser feels like a coach, not a tool.
Key message: Discipline is the edge. Your data tells the truth.

Growth Path

  • Month 1: Foundation and authority.
  • Month 2: Education + product awareness.
  • Month 3: Activation + trials.
  • Month 4: Community + retention.
  • Month 5: Scale via partnerships.
  • Month 6: Brand authority + PR.

Month-by-Month Execution

Clear monthly objectives with channel-level actions across SEO, Social, Founder, Influencers, Podcast, and Paid.

1

Month 1: Foundation + Positioning (Launch Month)

Establish brand, authority, and clarity. No aggressive selling. Only education and trust.

SEO setup
Social authority
Founder trust
No paid ads

SEO

Technical Setup

  • Title tags, meta descriptions, H1/H2 structure for core pages
  • Sitemap.xml + robots.txt
  • Schema for SaaS product + FAQs
  • Page speed optimization + internal linking structure

Keyword buckets: trading journal, trading discipline, options trading journal, zerodha trade tracking, trading analytics, trading journal excel

SEO Content

  • What is a trading journal and why most traders fail without one
  • Why discipline matters more than indicators
  • Best trading journal for Indian traders
  • How to review your trades like a professional
Goal is indexing and relevance, not traffic.

Social Media

Platforms: Instagram, YouTube Shorts, X, Facebook

Frequency

  • Instagram: 4 reels per week
  • YouTube Shorts: 4 per week
  • X: 1 post per day
  • Facebook: 3 per week

Content buckets: psychology, discipline mistakes, data-driven trading, founder content, light demos

Founder + Influencers

Founder Voice Series

  • 2 videos per week, 3 to 5 minutes
  • Topics: journey, why TradeLyser, psychology, discipline frameworks

Influencers: Identify 30 creators and engage consistently. No paid deals yet.

Paid ads: none. Do not spend before message-market fit.
2

Month 2: Education + Product Awareness

Make traders aware that a better way exists. Education stays first, product becomes visible.

Blogs + comparisons
More screen recordings
Retargeting only

SEO

  • 6 blog posts
  • 2 comparison pages: TradeLyser vs Excel, TradeLyser vs TraderSync
  • Start internal linking across all content

Sample topics: track options trades properly, review intraday trades, why Excel journaling fails, mistakes you repeat without knowing

Social Media

  • Instagram + YouTube: 5 reels per week
  • X: 2 posts per day + 1 thread per week
  • Shift: more screen recordings of strategy board, tagging, AI insights
Education first, product second. Keep the tone authoritative.

Founder Content

  • Weekly Trading Talk: 1 long video per week (10 to 15 mins)
  • Cut into 5 to 7 shorts
  • Topics: overtrading, emotions, pro reviews, what data reveals about mindset

Soft CTA: “If you are serious about improving, tools like TradeLyser exist for this exact reason.”

Influencers + Ads

Influencer outreach

  • 10 micro influencers (10k to 50k)
  • Offer free pro access, early feature access, revenue share
  • No heavy cash deals

Paid ads: Retargeting only (site visitors, profile visitors, video viewers). Small budget tests.

3

Month 3: Activation + Trial Push

Push trials and broker connections. Stronger product focus with proof-driven stories.

Broker pages
Use cases + stories
Cold traffic starts

SEO

  • 8 new blogs
  • 3 broker-specific pages (high intent)
  • Examples: Zerodha users, Groww users, Angel One users
Broker pages convert well because the intent is specific.

Social Media

Content mix

  • 40% education
  • 40% product use cases
  • 20% testimonials and user stories

Example angles: found losing strategy, one rule improved consistency, discipline score changed behavior

Influencer Demo Campaign

  • Each influencer: 1 full demo video
  • 2 short clips + 1 story set
  • Core message: “This is how I review my trades now.”
Trust scales here. This is the turning point.

Podcast + Paid Ads

Podcast guests: 1 trader, 1 mentor, 1 finance creator (conversational)

Paid ads: Start cold traffic on Google Search + Meta Reels

  • Keywords: trading journal, options trading journal, trading analytics, zerodha journal
  • Creatives: problem-based, not feature-based
4

Month 4: Community + Retention

Turn users into loyal users by building discipline habits and public progress.

Challenge
Habit loop
Case studies

Discipline Challenge

  • 4-week challenge
  • Journal daily, tag every trade, follow max loss rule
  • Weekly review

Rewards: free month, feature access, public recognition

This builds identity and long-term retention.

Social Media

  • Challenge updates
  • User progress stories
  • Behind-the-scenes content

Podcast

  • Review user journeys
  • Mistakes fixed using discipline
  • Lessons from weekly reviews

SEO + Influencers

  • Publish case studies and user stories
  • Invite influencers to join the challenge publicly
User stories rank and convert. Influencer participation multiplies reach.
5

Month 5: Scale + Partnerships

Lower CAC through broker and mentor distribution. Scale only what already works.

Partnerships
Mentor program
Performance scaling

Broker Partnerships

  • Co-webinars
  • Co-email campaigns
  • In-app placements
Message: “Trade better. Trade disciplined.”

Mentor Program

  • Target 20 mentors
  • Provide mentor dashboard, discount codes, marketing kit
  • Mentors bring mentees into TradeLyser
This creates a repeatable distribution loop.

Influencers

  • Sign 3 larger creators
  • Long-term contracts
  • Consistent demos + real review workflows

Paid Ads

  • Increase budget only on winning creatives
  • Kill everything else
  • Scale with proof and performance data
No emotional scaling. Only what works gets budget.
6

Month 6: Brand Authority + PR

Make TradeLyser a category name. Own the conversation around discipline in India.

PR
Media
TradeLyser Podcast

PR + Media

  • Founder interviews
  • Articles: trading discipline, AI in trading, Indian trader psychology
  • Thought leadership distribution on social + community
Objective: category association with discipline and performance analytics.

TradeLyser Podcast Launch

  • Weekly episodes
  • Guests: traders, mentors, psychologists, market experts
  • Repurpose each episode into short-form clips

Authority Outcomes

  • More organic branded searches
  • Higher conversion on trials due to trust
  • Creator and mentor ecosystem becomes a growth engine

Category Goal

TradeLyser is not another tool. It is the discipline layer for traders. The final outcome is strong positioning, strong proof, and repeatable distribution.

TradeLyser becomes the default name in the “trading discipline” conversation.
